TL;DR

What?

This is a site to read the Book of Mormon in two different languages of your choice. Simply choose your chapter, book and both of the languages and click "search".

Why?

I believe in the Gift of Tongues, and do know the Book of Mormon has power to help the reader achieve a higher proficiency in their target language. This comes of course, along your dedication as a language learner.

Suggestions & Improvements

All suggestions are welcome via GitHub issues. At the bottom you may find the link to the repository.

Improvements will come as long as the tool is being used. Please make sure to share it with your friends and local missionaries.
