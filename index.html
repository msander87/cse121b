How to play:

The game will show you the first 3 verses of the same chapter of one book of the book of Mormon. You have to guess which book it is and which chapter. If you get the name of the book right, you earn 1 point. If you get the name of the book right and the chapter you selected is just before or after the correct chapter, you earn 2 points. If you get the book and chapter right, you earn 3 points.
