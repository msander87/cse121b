INSTRUCTIONS

In order to play, you must create a username, this way your score will be saved. The next time you play you can create another username or choose the username you already had by logging in with the username and password you chose when creating your username.

The game round consists of 10 random questions, the questions will always be that you guess a character. For that, you will have some free hints. Each time a character is guessed you earn 10 points, but each time you use additional hints, points will be subtracted according to the hint you chose. If you want to skip that question, just click "skip this question", you will not gain or lose points. If you try to answer and fail, you won't lose points, but the question will be skipped.

The 10 best scores will be registered with the corresponding username in the "top scores" section. Keep in mind that the maximum score you can get is 100 points, and you can also get negative scores.

Put your knowledge into practice and have fun!
